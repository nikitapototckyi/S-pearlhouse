Benefits

Territorial proximity formed a single architectural ensemble, which is called the "Pearl Quarter" on French Boulevard. The internal comfort of the complex is provided by the best European systems, the operation of which is systematically monitored by specialized specialists.

Backup energy
and water supply

The complexes are completely autonomous. In case of a water or electricity cut-off, houses rely on their own supply. Such full-fledged, uninterrupted maintenance vitality of the house is an unchanging standard of the company

Own security service

The security service formed in each complex monitors order on the territory of the complex. Additional control over movement in public places of the building is carried out by a 24-hour video surveillance system. A comprehensive approach to the issue of security is an unchanging standard of the company

Fire safety system

A modern fire safety system is installed in all complexes. A fire cabinet is equipped on each floor. Public places are equipped with an automatic fire extinguishing system. In the event of a fire, the system can independently block the spread of smoke.

High-speed elevators

Fast and safe movement in the complexes is carried out due to the reliable elevator systems of the KONE company. They consume 50-70% less electricity compared to other elevators. They also have a low level of noise and vibration
